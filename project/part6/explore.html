Boardwalk Loop

This is an accessible, easy-to-follow, densely foliated, boardwalk loop located in Congaree National Forest. As you walk through the various flood plain forest habitats, look for pileated woodpeckers, barred owls, osprey, great blue heron, and a variety of other birds and other wildlife.

Mountain Vista Trail

Wander through a tapestry of diverse ecosystems, where the landscape transforms with every step you take. Experience the changing faces of nature as you traverse forests, meadows, and waterways, immersing yourself in the vibrant tapestry of wildlife and flora. This trail offers an educational and refreshing outdoor adventure, inviting you to appreciate the delicate balance of the natural world.

Waterfall Wonder

Explore rugged terrain on this adventurous trail, offering a more demanding and challenging hiking experience amidst a dramatic natural setting. Designed for seasoned adventurers seeking a physical challenge and immersive outdoor experience, this trail winds through captivating landscapes, requiring stamina and skill. Prepare to be rewarded with stunning vistas and a profound connection to the raw, untamed beauty of the wilderness
