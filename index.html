Problem Statement

University students are overwhelmed by too many class options, causing stress and poor course choices.

Affinity Diagram

We sorted varyings questions and problems in an attempt to formulate quality solutions for the issue of students being overwhelmed with options for classes to take. These include the problems of format, target users, school usage, and implementation methods such as mobile apps, websites, and other aspects such as ease of use and inclusivity. This resulted in an idea for a AI powered program that recommend students classes based on their enjoyment and performance in other classes.

Wireframe Sketches

Created 3 simple Wireframe sketches showcasing the utility of my app, "CourseCompass". This is done through showing things such as account creation, adding classes to get personalized recommendation using AI, and finally showing the difference that school administration could use to easily and efficiently manage students classes via their phone, and being able to get recommendations for each and every one of their students.

Prototype

Paper prototype demoing features of the AI powered course recommendation app.
