Problem Statement: Help homeless people and ex-convicts find jobs

Helping homeless individuals and ex-convicts find employment is a challenge that requires lots of time and resources from employers and the government.

Affinity Diagram: Job Finding for homeless people and ex-convicts

We sorted varyings questions and problems in an attempt to formulate quality solutions for the issue of homelessness in urban areas such as Columbia. These include the problems of location, government funding, transporation, application formats such as mobile apps and website, and other aspects such as the workplace enviornment or life outside of the workplace.

Team Personas: Community of Columbia

James Smith: "Anything that helps the community will end up helping me too"

Team Storyboards: Helping people across the community find jobs and opportunities that support them.

John Smith, a man that has just got out of jail, is struggling to find a job that will hire an ex ex-convicts. One of his friends tells him about this new app that is made for people such as him find a job that is willing to hire someone such as him. This culminates in John finding a job through the app and making enough money to get back on track with his finances.
